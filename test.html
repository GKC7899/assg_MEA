Game Changer

Game Changer is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language political action drama film directed by S. Shankar from a story written by Karthik Subbaraj. Produced by Sri Venkateswara Creations and Zee Studios, the film stars Ram Charan, alongside Kiara Advani, Anjali, Samuthirakani, S. J. Suryah, Srikanth and Sunil.
